Levels And Grade

We offer music instruction to students of all ages and skill levels,
from beginner to proficient. Our levels and grades are designed to provide
a clear pathway for progress and development...

BEGINNER

(Your Rhythmic Adventure)

1.Introduction to music theory and instrument basics.
2.Development off ndamental skills, such as hand positioning, finger placement, and basic techniques.
3.Simple songs and exercises to build confidence and familiarity.

INTERMEDIATE

(Your Rhythmic Adventure)

1.Expansion of technical skills, including scales, arpeggios, and chord progressions.
2.Introduction to more complex repertoire, including classical and contemporary pieces.
3.Focus on musical expression, phrasing, and dynamics.

ADVANCE

(Your Rhythmic Adventure)

1.Refining techniques, such as tone production, articulation, and pedaling (for pianists).
2.Exploration of advanced repertoire, including intricate compositions and nuanced interpretations.
3.Emphasis on performance skills, including stage presence and audience engagement.

PROFICIENT

(Your Rhythmic Adventure)

1.Mastery of instrument and musical style, with a focus on subtlety and finesse.
2.Development of personal expression and interpretation, with a deep understanding of the music.
3.Preparation for professional performances, auditions, or music competitions.

How do i know my grade?

Determining Your Grade

At your first lesson, our experienced instructors will:
  • Assess your current skills and knowledge
  • Evaluate your musical strengths and Areas for improvement
  • Discuss your musical goals and aspirations
  • Create a personalized learning plan tailored to your needs
This assessment will help determine your placement in our grade levels:
  • . Primer to Grade 2: Beginner level, focusing on fundamental skills and basic techniques
  • Grade 3-4: Intermediate level, expanding technical skills and introducing complex repertoire
  • Grade 5-7: Advanced level, refining techniques and exploring advanced repertoire
  • Grade 8 and above: Proficient level, mastering instrument and musical style, with a focus on subtlety and finesse

Recital Examination & Assesment

At the end of each session, both music theory and performance examinations are conducted. Regular evaluations and assessments are essential to a student's musical growth and development. As part of our efforts to ensure proper curriculum monitoring, progress level tracking and to ensure our students have a blossoming development in music appreciation, assessment evaluations are conducted monthly. Our recitals provide:

  • Performance Experience: Opportunities to perform in front of audiences, developing stage presence and confidence
  • Improved Skills: Recitals help students refine their musical skills, including technique, tone, and expression.
  • Motivation: Performing in recitals motivates students to practice and improve, setting goals and striving for excellence.
